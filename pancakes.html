Meatballs Pancakes

Pancakes for everyone

Intructions

Mix wheat, baking soda and salt in a bowl. Whip half of the milk and whip until it is smooth. Add the eggs, vanilla powder, melt the butter and the rest of the milk to the batter. Make the pancakes. Melt some butter in a hot frying pan. Pour 1/2-1 dl pancake batter in the pan, depending on how thick you want the pancakes to be. Serve them with whipped cream, fresh strawberries or yam.

Ingredients:

  • 2,5 dl wheat flour
  • 2,5 tablespoons baking soda
  • 0,5 teaspoons salt
  • 3 eggs
  • 6 dl milk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla powder
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
