Meatballs Pancakes

Some nice everyday meatballs


Intructions

Mix breadcrumbs with the milk, let it swell for 10 min. Stirr in the ground beaf and work until smooth. Add the eggs, salt, white pepper and onion. Add spices after your own taste. Form the balls or and cook them 5-8 min on medium high temperature.

Ingredients:

  • 400 g ground beef
  • 1,5 dl milk or water
  • 4 tablespoons breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 1,5 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoons white pepper
  • 2 tablespoons fried chopped onion
  • some sugar
